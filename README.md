# EminenceEdge

**Three-layer AI due diligence platform for professional services firms.**

This repository serves as a public architectural shell documenting the approach to EminenceEdge, an automated platform designed to ensure engagement consistency, regulatory compliance, and client intelligence at the point of pursuit and delivery.

## The Problem

Professional services firms are deploying AI inconsistently across engagements. Different teams use different tools, document different risks, and produce different deliverables for similar client situations. This creates regulatory exposure, quality variance, and missed commercialization opportunities.

## What It Does

EminenceEdge operates through three sequential layers:

1.  **Client/Prospect Intelligence:** Automated research and risk profiling at engagement intake.
2.  **Engagement Consistency:** Structured frameworks and reusable components ensuring delivery quality across teams.
3.  **Regulatory Compliance:** Automated checks against AI governance requirements relevant to each client's sector.

## Architecture

*   **Multi-Agent System:** Utilizing role-specialized LLM workflows to handle complex, multi-step due diligence tasks.
*   **Structured Output Validation:** Ensuring all AI-generated outputs adhere to strict formatting and quality standards required for professional services.
*   **Enterprise Integration Layer:** Connecting seamlessly with existing firm infrastructure and data sources.
*   **Audit Trail & Governance Logging:** Maintaining comprehensive records of all AI interactions and decisions for compliance and review.
